## Package Name
> A chain code was a type of identification marker that contained biographical information on an individual. Attuned to a subject's specific bio-metrics, these markers cataloged certain features of a person and their history. They were first introduced by the fledgling Galactic Empire shortly after the Clone Wars, and continued to be used by the New Republic. Chain codes were also commonly used by bounty hunters, who used the markers in conjunction with devices known as tracking fobs to locate their targets.
